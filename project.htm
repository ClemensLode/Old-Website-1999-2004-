Our Project

The game plays in a fantastic world with magic, mystics and strange animals. 
You can choose one of the intelligent races (like elves, human and dwarfes, even 
orcs and ogres). Every people does what HE wants to do. If you're evil (to the citizens)
no one will accept you as leader. 
You have to develop new technologies, research magic spells, explore the world, ...
In campaign mode you have to solve the mysterious past of the planet.

The game will be a realtime game which is interrupted at the end of every week to calculate several things like weather and new strategies of the enemy.
We tested several roleplay systems to create whole new rules which are very realistic and easy to understand. 
(A mixture of "DasSchwarzeAuge", "Ars Magica" and some self made games). 
The whole area will be completely in 3D, the objects in 2D.

From : Rules, Version Alpha 0.1

Overview

Working Title:  WARS!
Genre: Mix of building up, real time strategy, roleplay game, economy simulation
Publisher: Sierra canceled... waiting for new offers... :)
Graphic: 640*480, 800*600, 1024*768, 1280*1024 (3Dfx under construction)
Colors: 16 Bit
Goal: Complete control over economy, politic, religion, magic or military.
In campaign mode: Solve the questions about the past of the planet.
Features:
- Very intelligent economy system, every inhabitant works for himself to get much money, honor and many children.
- Every inhabitant has own way of life, preferences and qualities which don't directly depend on what they work.
- Science: Discover the plants, the resources, new technologies, magic, the people...
- A realistic environment with many different plants, animals and weather.
Several game modes:
- Roleplay-mode: Create a group of heroes and discover different worlds!
- Little-man-mode: Take the role of a single poor farmer and try to become powerful!
Convince the people, that you are the right king!
- Hack&Slay-mode: Just like WarCraft(TM) or C&C(TM), take control over some units and succed in many different missions!
- King-mode: the ultimative mode, with all possibilites you can have in WARS!

Different races:8-12
Number of different...
- abilities per unit: >50
- plants: >40
- animals: >40
- resources: >100 (every resource has 3 quality levels)
- jobs: >60
- buildings:
- 9 qualitiy levels
- up to 6 upgrades (size, height)
- >40 with different function

Platform: Win9X
Programming Language: C++
Size of Code at the moment: about 200.000 bytes
Beginning of project: Winter '98
